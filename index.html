RefundCheck Support

RefundCheck helps you keep track of pending and received refunds, expected arrival dates, and reminder notifications.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my information stored?

Refund records are stored locally on your device. RefundCheck does not connect to your bank account and does not automatically verify payments.

How do reminders work?

RefundCheck can send a local notification around the expected arrival date you enter. Notification permission must be enabled on your device.

How do I delete a refund?

Open the refund details page and select “Delete Refund,” or use the available swipe action from the refund list.
