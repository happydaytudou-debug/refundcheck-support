RefundCheck Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

RefundCheck is designed to help users manually track pending and received refunds. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

RefundCheck does not require account registration and does not collect personal information on external servers.

Information you enter into the app may include:

  • Merchant name
  • Refund amount and currency
  • Refund request date
  • Expected arrival date
  • Payment method
  • Optional notes

How Your Information Is Stored

Refund records are stored locally on your device. RefundCheck does not upload your refund records to our servers.

Banking and Payment Information

RefundCheck does not connect to bank accounts, payment cards, payment providers, or financial institutions. The app does not process payments and does not automatically verify whether a refund has arrived.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, RefundCheck may schedule local notifications based on the expected arrival date you enter. These notifications are generated on your device.

Third-Party Services

RefundCheck does not use third-party advertising, analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual refund records from within the app. You can also remove all locally stored app data by deleting RefundCheck from your device.

Children’s Privacy

RefundCheck does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app changes. Any updates will be posted on this page.
